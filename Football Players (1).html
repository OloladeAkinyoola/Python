A football player dataset typically contains detailed information about individual players' performance and statistics in football (soccer). The dataset usually includes a variety of attributes that capture different aspects of a player's involvement in matches, goals scored, assists, playing positions, disciplinary actions, and more. Such datasets are often used for analysis, statistical modeling, and gaining insights into player and team performance.

Here are common columns you might find in a football player dataset: 1 'Player': Name of the player. 2 'Team': The team the player is associated with. 3 'Nation': Nationality of the player. 4 'Pos': Position of the player. 5 'Age': Age of the player. 6 'MP': Matches played. 7 'Starts': Number of starts in matches. 8 'Min': Total minutes played. 9 '90s': Total minutes played divided by 90 (standard match duration). 10 'Gls': Goals scored. 11 'Ast': Assists. 12 'G-PK': Goals excluding penalty kicks. 13 'PK': Penalty kicks scored. 14 'PKatt': Penalty kicks attempted. 15 'CrdY': Yellow cards received. 16 'CrdR': Red cards received. 17 'Gls.1': Goals per 90 minutes. 18 'Ast.1': Assists per 90 minutes. 19 'G+A': Goals plus assists. 20 'G-PK.1': Goals excluding penalty kicks per 90 minutes. 21 'G+A-PK': Goals plus assists excluding penalty kicks. 22 'xG': Expected goals. 23 'npxG': Non-penalty expected goals. 24 'xA': Expected assists. 25 'npxG+xA': Non-penalty expected goals plus assists. 26 'xG.1': Expected goals per 90 minutes. 27 'xA.1': Expected assists per 90 minutes. 28 'xG+xA': Expected goals plus assists. 29 'npxG.1': Non-penalty expected goals per 90 minutes. 30 'npxG+xA.1': Non-penalty expected goals plus assists per 90 minutes.

The aim and objectives of the analysis based on player performance, team analysis, position analysis, expected goals analysis, minutes played, discipline, efficiency analysis, and age and performance can vary depending on the specific goals and context of the analysis. However, here are general objectives that such an analysis might aim to achieve:

  1. Player Performance:

    • Aim: Evaluate the overall performance of individual players.
    • Objectives: Identify top goal scorers, players with high assists, and those with efficient goal-to-minute ratios.

  2. Team Analysis:

    • Aim: Understand team dynamics and effectiveness.
    • Objectives: Determine teams with the highest total goals, most assists, average player age, defensive records, and disciplinary trends.

  3. Position Analysis:

    • Aim: Explore the performance variations across different playing positions.
    • Objectives: Identify the positions with the highest average goals per player and analyze the distribution of age among players in different positions.

  4. Expected Goals Analysis:

    • Aim: Assess how well players are performing compared to statistical expectations.
    • Objectives: Analyze the correlation between actual and expected goals, identify players exceeding their expected goals, and evaluate differences between actual and expected goals.

  5. Minutes Played:

    • Aim: Examine player involvement and playing time.
    • Objectives: Identify players with the most minutes played, calculate average starts, and assess player contributions based on playing time.

  6. Discipline:

    • Aim: Understand the disciplinary aspects of player behavior.
    • Objectives: Identify players with the most yellow and red cards, analyze team-wise average yellow cards, and explore the relationship between age and disciplinary actions.

  7. Efficiency Analysis:

    • Aim: Evaluate the efficiency of players in converting chances.
    • Objectives: Calculate the goals-to-expected-goals ratio, identify players with the highest non-penalty goals per 90 minutes, and find players with the best combined non-penalty goals and assists per 90 minutes.

  8. Age and Performance:

    • Aim: Explore the relationship between player age and performance metrics.
    • Objectives: Analyze the correlation between age and goals or assists, evaluate age distribution across different playing positions, and examine the relationship between age and disciplinary actions.

Overall, the analysis aims to provide insights into player and team performance, efficiency, and behavior, contributing to a better understanding of the factors influencing success in football. The specific objectives can be tailored based on the goals and interests of the analyst or researcher conducting the analysis.
